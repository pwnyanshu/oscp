- **Proxy** — Intercepts and inspects traffic.
    
- **Repeater** — Modify and resend requests.
    
- **Intruder** — Automates custom fuzzing attacks (Brute Force) .
    
- **Decoder** — Decodes and encodes data.
    
- **Comparer** — Diffs requests and responses.
    
- **Sequencer** — Analyzes randomness of tokens.
